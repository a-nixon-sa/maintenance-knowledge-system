Maintenance History

June 28, 2026

Photoeye Detection Failure

Symptom: Cases intermittently failed to register before entering the palletizer.

Root Cause: Dust accumulation on photoeye lens reduced detection accuracy.

Corrective Action: Cleaned sensor, verified alignment, and added lens cleaning to PM checklist.

Result: No repeat failures reported.

April 17, 2026

Discharge Conveyor Shutdown

Symptom: Conveyor unexpectedly stopped during pallet discharge.

Root Cause: Loose motor terminal inside electrical enclosure.

Corrective Action: Retorqued terminals and added annual torque verification to PM.

Result: Electrical fault eliminated.

January 5, 2026

Excessive Mechanical Vibration

Symptom: Vibration increased during pallet stacking.

Root Cause: Drive chain tension outside manufacturer specification.

Corrective Action: Chain adjusted and monthly inspection interval added.

Result: Vibration returned to acceptable range.
